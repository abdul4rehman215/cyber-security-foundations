Q1. What is security automation?
A. Automating updates, monitoring, and patching to reduce manual effort and risk.

Q2. Why is automated patching important?
A. It reduces exposure to known vulnerabilities.

Q3. Role of system-update.sh?
A. Full system update, cleanup, logging, reboot check.

Q4. Difference between system-update.sh and security-update.sh?
A. One updates all packages, the other focuses on security patches.

Q5. Why logging is critical?
A. Enables auditing, troubleshooting, and compliance.

Q6. What does system_monitor.py check?
A. Failed logins, disk usage, pending updates.

Q7. Why create system snapshots?
A. Rollback and audit safety.

Q8. How does cron help security?
A. Ensures consistent unattended maintenance.

Q9. Why use PowerShell on Linux?
A. Simulates Windows enterprise workflows.

Q10. SOC relevance?
A. Reflects real-world patching, monitoring, and automation tasks.
